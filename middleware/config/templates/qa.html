User Inquiry Guidelines

  • Inquiries about Specific Chapters: Clearly specify the chapter reference.
    Example: What is the main content of Chapter 2?
  • Inquiries regarding Image Content: Clearly indicate the image reference.
    Example: What is the main content of Image 0 in Chapter 2?
  • Note: Image references should be based on the text located below the image, rather than the internal numbering within the image.
